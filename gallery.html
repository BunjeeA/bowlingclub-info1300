Team

The Cornell Bowling Club is made up of a dedicated group of individuals who share a passion for the sport. Our team is composed of students from various backgrounds and academic disciplines, united by our love of bowling and commitment to representing Cornell University in the best possible light. We come from all over the world, bringing diverse perspectives and experiences to the team.

Despite our differences, we are a tight-knit group that supports one another both on and off the lanes. We value teamwork, communication, and sportsmanship, and strive to uphold these values in all aspects of our lives. Our team is led by dedicated coaches who provide us with guidance, support, and feedback to help us improve our skills and achieve our goals.
