Mission Statement

The mission of Cornell Bowling Club is to provide Cornell community members of all skill levels with an opportunity to bowl recreationally and/or competitively throughout their college careers. The Bowling Club also provides members with a fun, rewarding, and inclusive community on campus.

Our club provides a space for members to participate in bowling recreationally and/or competitively throughout their college careers. For those who are interested in pursuing competitive bowling, we offer a range of opportunities to compete in tournaments and represent Cornell University at the highest level. For those who are looking for a more casual experience, we offer regular practices and social events that allow members to enjoy the sport of bowling in a relaxed and fun atmosphere.

In addition to promoting the sport of bowling, our club is also dedicated to fostering a fun, rewarding, and inclusive community on campus. We believe that a strong sense of community is essential to our members' success both on and off the lanes. Through our regular practices, social events, and team-building activities, we work to build strong relationships between our members and promote a culture of teamwork and support.

Overall, the Cornell Bowling Club is committed to providing a safe, welcoming, and inclusive space for anyone who is interested in bowling at any level. We believe that everyone has something to gain from participating in sports and being part of a team, and we are proud to offer our members the opportunity to grow, learn, and succeed both on and off the lanes.
